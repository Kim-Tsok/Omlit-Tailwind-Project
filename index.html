What is Omlit?

Omlit is a solution to the problem of food wastage.
where someone in your local area puts up food that is currently in excess
or of no use to them for sale. From breakfast to lunch, if it is edible, it can be sold on omlit
which provides income for the seller on food that would have been wasted
